Native Dialog + Custom Popup

Two exit strategies on one page:

  • Custom HTML popup — move your mouse to the top of the window. Fully styleable, and its title/message are editable below.
  • Native dialog — try to reload or close the tab. Browser-controlled; its look and text can't be changed.

The custom popup is just HTML, so you can change anything — text, buttons, colors, layout. The native dialog can't be customized: browsers show their own wording (the text is still passed to e.returnValue for older browsers).
